How It Works

Getting Started

1
Browse Available Tasks

Go to Tasks page and select any task you want to complete. Each task shows how much you'll earn.

2
Copy the Review Comment

Click "Copy Comment" button. A positive review comment will be copied to your clipboard.

3
Visit the Link & Post Review

Click "Visit Link" button. It will open Google Maps. Paste the copied comment and post your review with 5 stars.

4
Wait for Timer (29 seconds)

After clicking the link, a 29-second timer will start. Wait for it to complete. This ensures you posted the review.

5
Upload Screenshot & Submit

Take a screenshot of your posted review. Upload it and click Submit. Admin will verify and credit money to your account!

Earning & Withdrawal

Task Approval

After you submit, admin will review your screenshot. If approved, money is instantly credited to your wallet!

Minimum Withdrawal

You can withdraw money when your balance reaches ₹50. Go to Wallet page and click Withdraw.

Referral Bonus

Share your referral link from Profile page. Earn ₹1 for every friend who signs up!
